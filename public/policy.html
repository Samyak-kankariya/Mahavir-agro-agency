Refund & Return Policy

Your satisfaction is important to us. Please read our policy carefully.

Returns

At Mahavir Agro Agency, we strive to ensure that our customers receive only the best quality products. However, if you are not satisfied with your purchase, we accept returns within 7 days of delivery, subject to the conditions below.

  • The item must be unused, unopened, and in its original packaging.
  • A valid proof of purchase (invoice or receipt) is required.
  • Returns must be initiated by contacting our support team.
